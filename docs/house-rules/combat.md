# Combat Rules

## Initiative and Timing

### HR-CO-001 — Side-Based Initiative

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Classic Traveller, modified  
**Rule:** Instead of individual initiative, combat uses side-based initiative. At the start of each combat, each side rolls 2d6 + highest Tactics skill in the group. Higher side acts first. Ties are resolved simultaneously.

---

### HR-CO-002 — Tactical Movement

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Player proposal  
**Rule:** A character who dashes (uses their entire movement allowance) suffers DM-1 to all attack and skill checks until the start of their next turn.

---

## Ranged Combat

### HR-CO-010 — Called Shots

**Status:** OPTIONAL  
**Source:** Traveller Companion, adapted  
**Rule:** A character may declare a called shot to target a specific location before rolling to attack. This inflicts DM-2 to the attack. On a hit, apply location-specific effects from the table below.

| Location | Effect |
|---|---|
| Head | Critical: unconscious, 1d6 rounds |
| Arm | Weapon dropped, -1 to DEX checks |
| Leg | Movement halved |
| Vitals | +1d6 damage on penetrating hit |

---

### HR-CO-011 — Suppressive Fire

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** House rule  
**Rule:** A character with an automatic weapon (auto 3+) may declare Suppressive Fire. No attack roll is made. All enemies in a 10m cone who do not find cover immediately make an END check (DM based on weapon auto rating: Auto 3 = DM0, Auto 4 = DM+1, Auto 5 = DM+2). Failure means they are pinned (cannot move or attack) until the end of their next turn.

---

## Melee Combat

### HR-CO-020 — Grappling

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Modified from Mongoose 2e  
**Rule:** To initiate a grapple, make an opposed STR or DEX check. Winner controls the grapple. A grappled character suffers DM-2 to all ranged attacks and cannot move. Breaking free requires a successful opposed STR check as a significant action.

---

## Damage and Wounds

### HR-CO-030 — Heroic Last Stand

**Status:** EXPERIMENTAL  
**Source:** Proposed by player, playtesting since Session 8  
**Rule:** When a character is reduced to all three physical attributes at 0 (dying), they may make a final END check (8+). On success, they remain conscious for one more round and may take a single significant action before falling unconscious. This cannot prevent death from massive damage (damage > 2x highest attribute remaining).
