# Character Creation Rules

## Core Attribute Generation

### HR-CC-001 — Alternative Attribute Allocation

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Modified from Classic Traveller  
**Rule:** Instead of the standard 2d6 method, players may optionally allocate **72 points** across the six attributes (STR, DEX, END, INT, EDU, SOC). No attribute may exceed 12 or fall below 2. This method is available once per campaign (cannot be re-used after death).

---

### HR-CC-002 — Background Skills Cap

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Table decision, Session 0 of Eclipsed Horizons  
**Rule:** Background skills are limited to a maximum of 3 skills, each at level 0. Players may choose from Education and Homeworld skill lists as normal.

---

### HR-CC-003 — Injury Recovery at Character Creation

**Status:** OPTIONAL  
**Source:** House rule  
**Rule:** If a character suffers a severe injury during the life path, they may spend a full career term (4 years) in recovery rather than continuing their career path. This grants no skills or aging rolls for that term.

---

## Aging

### HR-CC-010 — Delayed Aging

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Player proposal, approved Session 3  
**Rule:** Aging rolls do not begin until the character has completed **4 career terms** (rather than 3). The standard aging table applies after that point.

---

### HR-CC-011 — Anagathics Availability

**Status:** EXPERIMENTAL  
**Source:** Proposed by referee, playtesting since Session 12  
**Rule:** Anagathics are available on worlds with TL 12+ at Cr5000/month (half the book price) but require a monthly Medical check (Effect 0+) or lose one year of reduced aging benefit.

---

## Connections

### HR-CC-020 — Extended Connections

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Traveller Companion adaptation  
**Rule:** Each player character may establish up to 3 connections with other PCs during character creation (instead of the standard 2). Each connection grants a contact or ally in addition to any bonus skill.
