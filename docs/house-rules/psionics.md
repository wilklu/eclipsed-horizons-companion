# Psionics Rules

## Psionic Talent Discovery

### HR-PS-001 — Hidden Potential

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** House rule  
**Rule:** Any character may attempt to discover latent psionic potential during play by visiting a Psionic Institute (available on TL 10+ worlds with appropriate government type). The base PSI score is 2d6. Characters who tested before age 18 (before the campaign started) may roll with DM+2.

---

## Psionic Strengths

### HR-PS-010 — PSI Attribute Refresh

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Table consensus  
**Rule:** PSI points recover at a rate of 1 per hour of rest (instead of full recovery after sleep). Full recovery still requires 8 hours of uninterrupted sleep/rest.

---

### HR-PS-011 — PSI Overload

**Status:** EXPERIMENTAL  
**Source:** Referee proposal, playtesting  
**Rule:** When a psion spends their last PSI point, they must roll on the PSI Overload table (2d6):

| Roll | Effect |
|---|---|
| 2-3 | Severe: Comatose 1d6 hours, lose 1 PSI permanently |
| 4-5 | Moderate: Unconscious 1d6 rounds, DM-2 to all checks for 1 hour |
| 6-8 | Minor: DM-1 to all checks until 1 PSI recovers |
| 9-11 | No effect beyond exhaustion |
| 12 | Surge: Next psionic power used in 10 minutes costs 0 PSI |

## Telepathy

### HR-PS-020 — Surface Thoughts vs. Deep Scan

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Book rule clarification  
**Rule:** Reading Surface Thoughts (as written) reveals only the target's current conscious focus. A Deep Scan (spending 3 PSI, difficulty 10+) allows the psion to probe for specific information. The referee rolls secretly; the player does not know if false information was encountered.
