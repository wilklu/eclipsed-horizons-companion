# World Building Rules

## Atmosphere Generation

### HR-WB-001 — Tainted Atmospheres

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Referee design, setting-specific  
**Rule:** When generating a Tainted atmosphere (code 2, 4, 7, or 9), the referee rolls 1d6 to determine taint type: 1-2 Chemical, 3 Biological, 4 Particulate, 5 Radioactive, 6 Exotic. Each type has different filter requirements and exposure effects.

## Population and Government

### HR-WB-010 — Civil War Worlds

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** House rule  
**Rule:** Any world with Population 7+ and Government code 0, 7, or inconsistency between Law Level and Government type (e.g., Law 9 + Government 1) may be in civil conflict. The referee rolls 1d6 — on a 5+ the world is experiencing active conflict, which affects all social and trade interactions.

## Tech Level Variations

### HR-WB-020 — Tech Level Pockets

**Status:** OPTIONAL  
**Source:** Adapted from various supplements  
**Rule:** On any world with TL 5+, the referee may declare that specific industries or settlements operate at TL±2 from the world's nominal TL. A TL 8 world might have a TL 10 research enclave or TL 6 frontier settlements. This reflects the uneven distribution of technology in a realistic economy.
