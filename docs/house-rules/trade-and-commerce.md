# Trade & Commerce Rules

## Speculative Trade

### HR-TC-001 — Extended Purchase Negotiation

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** Adapted from Traveller Companion  
**Rule:** A character with Broker 1+ may spend additional time (1d6 days) researching the local market before purchasing speculative cargo. If they do, they roll **twice** on the modified price table and take the better result. This can only be done once per port visit.

---

### HR-TC-002 — Black Market Access

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** House rule  
**Rule:** Characters with Streetwise 2+ or appropriate contacts may access black market goods on worlds with Law Level 6+. Black market goods trade at 1d6×10% above standard prices, but bypass Law Level restrictions. Getting caught carries significant legal risk (determined by referee).

---

## Freight and Passengers

### HR-TC-010 — Passenger Rumors

**Status:** OPTIONAL  
**Source:** Classic Traveller tradition  
**Rule:** For each high or middle passage passenger, the referee may secretly roll 1d6. On a 5+, that passenger carries a rumor or plot hook relevant to the current subsector. The referee introduces this organically during the voyage.

---

## Shipyard and Maintenance

### HR-TC-020 — Emergency Repairs

**Status:** APPROVED  
**Source:** House rule  
**Rule:** Instead of waiting for full maintenance, a ship with maintenance overdue may receive emergency patch repairs at 200% of standard maintenance cost. This resets the monthly maintenance clock but does not count as a full annual overhaul.
